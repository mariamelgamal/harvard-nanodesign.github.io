Mariam is a 5th year PhD candidate, co-advised by Professors Gage Hills and David Brooks. Her research interests are at the intersection of computer architecture and circuit design, with a focus on designing and optimizing computing systems for energy efficiency and environmental sustainability. Prior to Harvard, Mariam graduated with a double major in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from NYU. You can find more details on [her website](https://mariamelgamal.com/).
